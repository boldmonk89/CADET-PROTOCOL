22 points

Canines (4 teeth × 1 pt) 1 pt each
Condition: corresponding lower teeth must be present
1st & 2nd Premolars (8 teeth × 1 pt) 1 pt each
Condition: corresponding lower teeth must be present
1st & 2nd Molars (4 teeth × 2 pts) 2 pts each
Condition: must be in good opposition to corresponding lower jaw teeth
3rd Molar (Wisdom) — well developed 2 pts
3rd Molar (Wisdom) — underdeveloped 1 pt
'Well-opposed' means tooth from upper jaw makes functional contact with lower jaw counterpart in biting position
Severe pyorrhoea, untreated active caries, or mobile teeth = disqualification even if 14-point threshold is met. All counted teeth must be sound and repairable.
